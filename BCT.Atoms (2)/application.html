Admission Requirements

Requirements Needed:

  • College Freshmen: Form 138 (Grade 12), Good Moral, Birth Certificate (photocopy), and 2 copies of 2x2 ID pictures.
  • College Transferees: Transfer Credentials, True Copy of Grades, Good Moral Certificate, Birth Certificate (photocopy), and 2 copies of 2x2 ID pictures.
  • Senior High School: Original Form 138, PSA Birth Certificate, Good Moral Certificate, Certificate of Junior High Completion, and 2 copies of 2x2 ID pictures.
  • Note: No entrance examination required.
